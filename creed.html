Creed Upscale Bistro and Bar

Creed is located at No 31 Apara Rd, Elechi 500272, Port Harcourt. It’s a spacious outdoor spot with simple decor, perfect for family and group hangouts. They are opening from Monday to Sunday 12noon. It’s also nice spot to chill on Friday/Saturday nights, host your bridal shower, baby shower, girls hangout and all so you might want to keep an eye out for that on their Instagram @creed_portharcourt
